Founder and Ceo
AttireSoft, Inc.

Harald Rudell is the Founder and Ceo of AttireSoft, Inc. a company active in business software for online and devices.

Harald holds a Master of Science in Business Administration and Economics from Stockholm University and a Master of Science in Electrical Engineering, specializing in computer systems engineering, from The Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm, Sweden.

Mr. Rudell possesses broad knowledge and a multi-faceted education across business, engineering and law. This has enabled him several accomplishments:

  • Entrepreneurship
    • Three computer-related businesses in Stockholm Sweden.
    • Software business in California, US.
    • Working across start-up, pre-ipo to large corporations.
  • Internet Technologies
    • Cloud computing using all major providers.
    • Software architecture from C to JavaScript.
    • Enterprise class service design.
  • Complex Project Execution
    • Porting a 100-million monthly users service to Apple iOS.
    • On-site sponsor for a cellular site planning project of a European capital city.
    • US nationwide successful and timely roll-out of a top-four carrier overlay network.
  • Major Account Mobile Systems Sales
    • Closing numerous contracts ranging from usd 300 to 10 million.
    • Sole decision maker for a usd 40 million sale.
    • Living on three continents, traveling 49 countries.
